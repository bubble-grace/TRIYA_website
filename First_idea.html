Who are we?

TRIYA’s aim is to make sailing accessible to all school age students to encourage a love for the water and sailing. We run programs from beginner levels all the way to national champions. There are three main programs more information can be found on the relevant pages however these descriptions will give a brief overview on what program may suit your sailor.

420 Sailing

420's are internationally recognised boats, as the younger sibling of the 470 an olympic class. They are great for learning a developing skills in two people boats and are the perfect stepping stone from smaller boats such as starlings or open skiffs.
