There are three main programs more information can be found on the relevant pages however these descriptions will give a brief overview on what program may siut your sailor.

Learn to Sail

This is on weekdays from 4pm until 8pm and focuses on the basics of sailing learning how to tack, gybe, position in the boat and rigging. Currently this runs everyday after school apart from a Wednesday and is perfect for gaining confidence in and arround a 420 boat.

Teams Sailing

This is for more experienced sailors who are interested in tactics, teams racing consists of 3 boats (2 people per boat) per team and two teams, they complete in a short course race(less than 15 minutes long). The team that wins is the one that gets the most amount of boats over the line first, TRIYA is aiming to have 3 teams compete in the regionals, 1 from Wellington College, 1 open team and 1 from Scots Collge.

Fleet Racing

This is for sailors aiming to compete in Nationals in 2023 who are graduates of one of the sailing programs offered, each boats consists of a skipper and a crew. The crew will learn how to trapeze and mange a kite, and the skipper will be in charge of steering and controls to effect the main sail. Fleet racing sails on a Saturday with training in the morning and racing in the afternoon.
