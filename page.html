Provided Gear

TRIYA wants all students to have the opportunity to sail, to make this possible gear is available in the shed. This gear is Life jackets, rain jackets and trapeze harnesses to keep everyone warm and safe. The trapeze harnesses are only for crew however everyone must wear a life jacket.

Windy

Windy is a website seen to the side, this is used by many sailors to check the wind and how gusty it might get. Normally if it is gusting over 30 knots we do not sail due to the high chance of damage to sailors or boats. To see the wind just click on the location on the map, and it will bring up the weather forecast for the next week.

Gear

With gear we want to have a solid base to add more warm layers to, remember the wind chill brings the temperature down a few degrees. A wetsuit is recommended in summer and is a necessity if sailing in winter, it is always better to have more layers than too little, we don't want to come in early.

Repairs

If it is to windy we often repair or fix the boats in the shed, TRIYA owns 8 boats therefore is constantly fixing or upgrading boats. Ths Shed has tools to use and spare parts such as rope and shackles however it is helpful if you bring a tape measure, a knife and a ruler with the end a zero
